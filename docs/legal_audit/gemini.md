Seller Specific Terms1. Seller EligibilitySellers must be private persons resident in Estonia, Latvia, or Lithuania. Sellers must declare they are not acting in a professional capacity. If a Seller completes more than 30 transactions or earns more than €2,000 in a calendar year, they will be classified as a "Reportable Seller" .2. Tax Reporting (DAC7)Under EU Directive 2021/514 (DAC7), the Platform is legally required to collect and report Seller tax data (Personal Code/TIN, address, date of birth) to the Latvian State Revenue Service (VID) once the reportable thresholds are met . Non-compliance: If requested data is not provided within 60 days, the Platform must withhold all payouts to the Seller and/or close the account .3. Payouts and EveryPay KYCPayouts are made according to the schedule selected in the Seller Dashboard (e.g., weekly). To receive payouts, Sellers must complete the mandatory KYC (Know Your Customer) process via the EveryPay dashboard provided by the Platform . Payouts may be placed "on hold" in case of suspected fraud or transaction disputes .4. Shipping Liability (Unisend)
